Register your courses with UVic

  1. Login to UVic's Student services
  2. Navigate to Add or drop classes.
  3. Select the {{term}} term.
  4. Copy one by one the CRN's below.
  5. Paste the CRN's into the bottom boxes.
  6. Click Add classes.
  7. Repeat steps 4 - 6 for remaining CRN's.
